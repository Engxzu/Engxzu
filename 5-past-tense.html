    V. Simple Past Tense

    Suatu pekerjaan atau peristiwa yang berlaku pada masa lampau (masa lalu).

    Rumus:

    Bukan kata kerja:
    SUBJECT + TO BE (WAS, WERE ) + OBJECT
    Kata-Kerja:
    SUBJECT + VERB. II + OBJECT

    Contoh Kalimat:

    1.
    (+): I was a teacher
    Saya adalah seorang guru
    (-): I was not a teacher
    Saya bukan seorang guru
    (?): Was I a teacher?
    Apakah saya seorang guru?
    2.
    (+): He got much money
    Dia memperoleh banyak uang
    (-): He did not get much money
    Dia tidak mendapat banyak uang
    (?): Did he get much money?
    Apakah dia mendapat banyak uang?
    3.
    (+): The teacher wrote on the black board
    Guru menulis di papan tulis
    (-): The teacher did not write on the black board
    Guru tidak menulis di papan tulis
    (?): Did teacher write on the black board?
    Apakah guru menulis di papan tulis?
    4.
    (+): The students played in the school yard
    Pelajar-pelajar bermain di halaman sekolah
    (-): The students did not play in the school yard
    Pelajar-pelajar tidak bermain di halaman sekolah
    (?): Did the students play in the school yard?
    Apakah pelajar-pelajar bermain di halaman sekolah?
     
    5.
    (+): Ali went to Jakarta
    Ali berangkat ke Jakarta
    (-): Ali did not go to Jakarta
    Ali tidak berangkat ke Jakarta
    (?): Did Ali go to Jakarta?
    Apakah Ali berangkat ke Jakarta?
